You have a PDF with 10 pages but you only need page 3 as an image — to upload to a form that demands a JPG, to attach to a social post, or to insert into a slide deck. Most online "PDF to JPG" converters are slow, watermarked, file-size limited, and require uploading your PDF to a server.

Here's how to do it on your phone, instantly, for free.

## Quick answer

Open **[Scanvo](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.nexbrothers.scanvo)**, tap **Tools → PDF to JPG**, pick your PDF, choose which pages you want, and tap **Convert**. You get individual JPG files saved to your phone, one per page, ready to share or upload.

- **WhatsApp sharing** — JPG previews on WhatsApp; PDF doesn't
- **Quick reference** — JPGs open instantly in any phone gallery; PDFs need a reader

A native phone app makes this fast and lossless — no uploads, no waits, no watermark.

## Step-by-step guide

### Step 1: Install Scanvo
Free, offline, no signup. Get it from the Play Store.

[Download Scanvo on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.nexbrothers.scanvo)

### Step 2: Open the PDF to JPG tool
From the Scanvo home screen, tap **Tools**. Tap **PDF to JPG**.

### Step 3: Pick the PDF
Tap **Select PDF**. Your phone's file picker opens. Find the PDF — Downloads, Scanvo library, WhatsApp Documents, anywhere accessible. Tap to select.

### Step 4: Choose pages to convert

Scanvo shows you a thumbnail grid of every page in the PDF. You have three options:

- **Convert all pages**: tap **Select All**
- **Convert specific pages**: tap each page you want (e.g. just page 3 and page 7)
- **Convert a range**: tap the first page, then long-press the last page in the range

This is much more flexible than online tools that usually convert the entire PDF.

### Step 5: Pick output settings
Tap the gear icon for options:

- **Image quality**: High (sharpest, larger file), Medium (balanced), Low (smaller, slight blur on small text)
- **Image format**: JPG (smaller, universal) or PNG (larger, lossless)
- **Resolution**: Fit to original page size (default), or specify a custom DPI

For most use cases, **JPG at Medium quality** is the right choice. For text-heavy pages where readability matters, switch to **High**.

### Step 6: Tap Convert
Scanvo extracts each selected page as a separate image. For typical PDFs (10 pages), this takes 1-3 seconds.

### Step 7: Save or share

Each converted JPG is shown in a results screen. From here you can:

- **Tap Save All** to save every JPG to your phone's gallery
- **Tap an individual page** to save or share just that one
- **Tap Share** to send all JPGs at once via email, WhatsApp, or any other app

The originals are saved with names like `MyDocument_page1.jpg`, `MyDocument_page2.jpg`, etc.

## Pro tips

- **For slides or presentations**, convert at High quality to keep text crisp when projected
- **For WhatsApp sharing**, convert at Medium and the JPGs will compress well within WhatsApp's image preview
- **For social media**, crop the JPG after conversion to fit Instagram (1:1) or Twitter (16:9) aspect ratios — Scanvo has a built-in crop tool in the editor
- **To convert a single page**, you don't need to extract all pages — Scanvo lets you select just that one
- **To convert and re-merge** (e.g. extract pages 3-5 and combine them with another PDF), use PDF to JPG → JPG to PDF → Merge PDF in sequence
- **For OCR after conversion**, you can drop the JPG back into Scanvo and run OCR to extract text from it

## Frequently asked questions

**Will the JPG be lower quality than the original PDF?**
At High quality, the difference is invisible. JPG compression introduces some lossiness, but for typical PDFs (text + images), it's not noticeable. For pixel-perfect output, use PNG instead.

**Can I convert a password-protected PDF?**
You'll need to unlock it first using **Tools → Unlock PDF** (you must know the password). Then convert.

**Is there a file size or page count limit?**
No. Scanvo handles PDFs of any size, limited only by your phone's RAM and storage.

**Will the converted JPGs have a watermark?**
No. Scanvo never adds watermarks.

**Can I convert PDFs from email attachments?**
Yes — open the attachment in your email app, tap Save (or Share → Save to Files), then open it in Scanvo via the file picker.

**What if my PDF is mostly text? Wouldn't it be better as text?**
If you need editable text rather than an image, use Scanvo's **OCR** feature instead. It extracts the text from any PDF or image.

**Will Scanvo upload my PDF when converting?**
No. Every step is offline. Your PDF and the resulting JPGs never leave your phone.

**Can I convert and rotate at the same time?**
Yes. After conversion, open any JPG in Scanvo's editor and rotate, crop, or adjust as needed.
